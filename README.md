## Manny Aziba

**System Translator for Developer-Built Products**

I analyze and redesign interfaces for developer-built products using structured UI systems drawn from CPSA's architecture.

Most developer-built products work technically. They lose users structurally. That is the gap I close.

---

## What I Do

| Lens | The question it asks |
|------|----------------------|
| **Structure** | Is the information organised in the right order? |
| **Flow** | Does the user always know where to go next? |
| **Feedback** | Does the interface communicate what is happening? |
| **Readability** | Can the user understand this without effort? |
| **Consistency** | Does this interface behave like one thing? |

---

## Who I Work With

**Indie hackers** who launched an MVP and cannot hold users past day three.

**SaaS founders** whose product works but whose retention does not.

**Developer agencies** whose client products need a coherent UI system layer.

**Early-stage startups** preparing a functional prototype for investment — and needing an interface that reads as credible.

The common thread: a product that functions technically and fails perceptually.

---

## Offers

**UX Intelligence Audit** — A documented, structured breakdown of where your interface loses users and why. Delivered within 48–72 hours. Not vague feedback. A specific diagnosis with a prioritised fix list.

**Interface Rework** — Structural redesign of the flows and components the audit identifies. One problem, resolved properly.

*The audit is the entry point. The rework follows from it.*
